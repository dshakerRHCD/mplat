My website is about Warwick, RI. This centrally located region, whose airport was named the third-best in America by the readers of Conde Nast Traveler, includes a diverse range of attractions and restaurants, as well as convenient shopping and lodging. Don’t overlook the area’s scenic beauty, beaches, and parks, which are some of the state’s best. (visitrhodeisland.com)

Some Cool Facts about Warwick!

  1. Rhode Island’s second largest city
  2. Established in 1642
  3. Population: 82,999

From historic sites and beautiful beaches to world-class dining and a terrific variety of retail and specialty shops, to top-notch recreational facilities and lodging options to suit every budget, Warwick has it all! https://visitwarwickri.com/
